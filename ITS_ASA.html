Modelling ITS

Main assumptions

  • Assumption 1: The expectation of the pre intervention level and trend would be the same irrespective of whether the sample received the treatment
  • Assumption 2: In the absence of the intervention, the post intervention trendline would have been equivalent in expectation to an extrapolated pre intervention trend.

Modelling ITS

  • RDD in time

    • Time-series variation for identification within the same unit of observation (doctors or patients)
    • Treatment impact may take time to develop and not be visible right at the time of the intervention
    • Seasonalities and trends

My take on existing literature

  • Causal problem well understood
  • Because it is used in different fields, concepts have different names but are overlapping
    • CITS vs DiD
    • ITS and RDDiT
  • Most skepticism due to the statistical uncertainty generated by projections (modeling)
